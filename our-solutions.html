Our Solutions

Our proprietary cutting-edge technology is designed for multi-platforms.

Fleet Management

We manage and support a maintenance program that is tailored to individual aircraft models and fleets. Individual maintenance tasks associated with a program can be defined and setup with the relevant applicability, life information (including thresholds), and intervals. The maintenance task and program revision process are supported through comparison tools, enabling users to easily identify and assess changes before they are implemented.

Information supporting the effective planning of scheduled maintenance can be defined beneath the program at task level. This information includes a variety of task control attributes and data that will facilitate the effective execution of tasks.

Cargo Management

Efficient Cargo Management Solutions for Seamless Operations

The Covid-19 pandemic has prompted significant change in the air cargo industry. Among other noticeable trends, the pandemic has highlighted the need to accelerate digital transformation across the industry. As the cargo industry recovers, businesses must proactively adapt to unpredictable cargo volumes, evolving client expectations, and heightened competitiveness.

Ikigai Innovation’s mission is to transform how cargo companies operate in a digital world by delivering next-generation technology that accelerates growth, drives efficiency, and creates differentiated customer experiences.

Our expertise is rooted in decades of dedicated service to the cargo industry and a proven record of successful implementation of previous software. Our cargo management systems accelerate innovation by enabling smarter, data-driven decisions for airlines and cargo agents.

Cargo Agent Portal

Ikigai’s Cargo Agent Portal is a fully automated online booking engine. Expediting booking requests all the way through to payments, our platform facilitates the entire transaction. By simply inputting your details: origin & destination, weight(s), dimensions, and flight date; you can book a cargo slot on an aircraft, with confirmation from the airline. The integration of the Agent Portal with the Airline’s Operations enables the booking process to be completed within minutes rather than days, with little to no paperwork.

In-Flight Management

We designed our automated handling systems to enhance in-flight catering facilities and perform beyond client expectations. We offer a touchless experience through our android and IOS app-based solution, enabling passengers to place their orders and pay electronically pre- flight or at the gate. Our integrated solution optimizes the system's performance to streamline operations and minimize operational costs.
